# BACKYARD BAYOU

## PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Titan Ventures Media LLC ("Company," "we," "our," or "us"), handles information in connection with the video game Backyard Bayou (the "Game"). It is incorporated by reference into the Backyard Bayou End User License Agreement ("EULA").

This Policy is designed to be plain and short. We collect only what we need to debug and improve the Game, we do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third-party advertisers.

---

### 1. WHO WE ARE

Titan Ventures Media LLC is a Virginia-based independent game studio. Backyard Bayou is developed and distributed by us through the Steam platform operated by Valve Corporation. For questions about this Policy, contact us at the email address in Section 13.

### 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

The Game collects the following limited categories of technical diagnostic information for development purposes only:

**(a) System and Hardware Information**, including:

- Operating system version
- CPU model
- GPU model and driver version
- System memory available to the Game
- Graphics renderer in use (e.g., Vulkan Forward+)
- Display resolution and refresh rate
- Game version and build number

**(b) Game Diagnostic Information**, including:

- Crash reports and stack traces
- Error messages and warning logs generated by the Game
- The contents of the Game's own internal console log, which records technical events emitted by the Game's code (for example: loading errors, performance warnings, system state at the time of a bug, and similar developer-facing diagnostics)
- Performance and frame-time metrics
- In-game session duration and gameplay events relevant to debugging (for example: which scene was loaded when a crash occurred, what action triggered an error)

This information is logged locally by the Game during play. Crash reports and diagnostic logs may be transmitted to us automatically or with your consent when a crash or significant error occurs.

### 3. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect or store:

- Your real name, mailing address, phone number, or email address
- Your Steam ID, Steam username, or Steam friends list
- Your payment information, credit card details, or billing information
- Your precise geographic location (no GPS, no IP geolocation tracking)
- Your IP address as a stored identifier
- Your browsing history, files, or any data outside the Game itself
- Voice or video recordings
- Biometric data
- Information about minors

Payment processing, account authentication, refund processing, and any related personal information are handled exclusively by Valve Corporation through Steam, and are governed by Valve's own privacy policies, which you can review at https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/.

### 4. HOW WE USE THIS INFORMATION

We use the diagnostic information described in Section 2 solely for the following purposes:

- Diagnosing and fixing bugs, crashes, and errors
- Identifying performance problems on specific hardware configurations
- Improving compatibility across systems
- Understanding which features cause the Game to behave incorrectly
- Improving overall stability and quality of the Game

We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, behavioral targeting, or to make decisions about individual players.

### 5. SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing partners.

We may disclose diagnostic information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers.** If we use a crash reporting or error logging service (such as a third-party diagnostic tool) to receive, store, or analyze the diagnostic information described in Section 2, that provider acts on our behalf and is bound to use the data only for the purposes we direct.
- **Legal requirements.** We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or valid legal process.
- **Business transfer.** If we sell or transfer the Game or our business, diagnostic information may be transferred as part of that transaction. Any successor will be bound by this Policy or a substantially similar one.

### 6. DATA RETENTION

We retain diagnostic information only as long as is reasonably necessary for the purposes described in Section 4. As a general rule:

- Crash reports and error logs: up to 24 months after receipt
- Performance and session diagnostic logs: up to 12 months after receipt
- Aggregated, non-identifying statistics derived from the above: may be retained indefinitely for development reference

We may delete or anonymize this information sooner at our discretion.

### 7. SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect the diagnostic information we collect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Game is rated for Teen audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us at the email address in Section 13 and we will take steps to delete it.

### 9. INTERNATIONAL PLAYERS

The Game is operated from the United States. If you access the Game from outside the United States, you understand that your diagnostic information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. By using the Game, you consent to such transfer.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with comprehensive data protection laws, you may have additional rights under local law. Please see Section 10 for the rights we honor regardless of your location.

### 10. YOUR RIGHTS

Regardless of where you are located, you may contact us at the email address in Section 13 to:

- Ask what information, if any, we hold that is associated with your specific Steam purchase or installation of the Game
- Request that we delete diagnostic information associated with your Steam purchase or installation, where doing so is technically feasible
- Ask questions about how your information is used

Because we do not collect identifying information such as your name, email address, or Steam ID by default, our ability to locate information associated with a specific individual is limited. In some cases, we may be unable to verify or fulfill a request without additional identifying information that you provide voluntarily.

### 11. STEAM'S ROLE

Valve Corporation, the operator of Steam, has its own privacy practices governing the collection and use of your Steam account information, payment details, and platform activity. This Policy does not cover Valve's practices. Please review the Steam Privacy Agreement at https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/ for information about Valve's data handling.

### 12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Material changes will be communicated through the Game's Steam store page, in-game notice, or other reasonable means. Your continued use of the Game after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
